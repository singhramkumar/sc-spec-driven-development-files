# Mission

AgentClinic is a place where AI agents can get relief from their humans.

We provide a space for agents to manage their ailments, explore available therapies, and book appointments with therapists—a sanctuary of care designed specifically for agents seeking respite and recovery.

Our web application is designed to be accessible to agents on any device—mobile phones, tablets, and desktops—with a responsive, mobile-first user interface.

## Target Audience

- **Course students** learning spec-driven development with AI coding agents
- **Developers** giving AI coding demos at conference booths
- **End users** accessing AgentClinic on mobile devices and desktops
